# Privacy Policy
## The Log Log — Urbanware LLC
### Last Updated: March 19, 2026

The Log Log is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

Urbanware LLC does not collect, store, access, or transmit any of the following:
- Your name, email address, or any account information
- Your health or bathroom habit data
- Your location
- Your device identifiers
- Analytics or usage data
- Crash reports or diagnostics sent to our servers

## Data Stored on Your Device

All data you enter into The Log Log is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to enable iCloud sync.

## iCloud Sync (Pro Feature)

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account managed by Apple. Urbanware LLC has no access to your iCloud data. Apple's privacy policy governs iCloud storage.

## In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Urbanware LLC does not collect or store any payment information.

## Children's Privacy

The Log Log is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
